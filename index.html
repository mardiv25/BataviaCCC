EXCELLENCIES

What We Offer

Trusted Spaces

Trusted Relationships is a government pilot tackling serious youth violence. We are sure that every young person we work with has a key, trusted adult in their lives who they can turn to in times of need - 24 hours a day, seven days a week

Fosters

We have foster carers living across Central Jakarta, West Jakarta, Tangerang, Bekasi and Bogor. We can provide different types of foster placements, including solo placements, therapeutic placements and sibling placements. Our carers are passionate about providing the best standard of caer to the children they look after.

Staying Close

Our Staying Close service aims to improve the experiences of young people moving from residential care to independence. We provide a stable home, opportunities to maintain important relationships, and tailored support. SERVICES

We Provide Better

Accommodation and Support

We provide safe, comfortable homes for care leavers, homeless teenagers and unaccompanied asylum-seeking children, where they can learn the essential life skills they will need when they transition to independent living.

Young Mentors

Our team of mentors supports young people aged 10-14. Mentors provide ten sessions within the community, taking time to get to know young people by working in a relationship-based way. We find out about their experiences and ambitions, linking them up with other community services and taking part in activities alongside them.

Children's Homes

Young people have their say on decorating their bedrooms, deciding house rules and picking what's on the menu each week. By empowering them to make decisions on issues that directly affect their lives, they feel more at home and positive about engaging with staff.

Safe Steps

A residential home for girls at risk, based in the local community. The girls can maintain and repair the postive relationships in their lives. They leave our care with a better understanding of sexual health and how they deserve to be treated.
